Hiring & Team Building Systems

You cannot scale if you cannot hire. Every service business owner knows the pain of a bad hire -- the wasted time training someone who quits in three weeks, the customer complaints from an employee who was not a fit, the constant feeling of being short-staffed. The problem is usually not that good people do not exist. It is that you do not have a system to find them, evaluate them, onboard them, and keep them. We build the hiring and team-building infrastructure that turns staffing from your biggest headache into a repeatable process.

Who This Is For

Owners who are stuck because they cannot find, keep, or develop the people they need to grow.

  • You have hired people who did not work out and you are not sure why
  • Your onboarding process is basically "follow someone around for a few days"
  • You are doing work that should be delegated but you do not trust anyone else to do it right
  • Roles are unclear -- people do a little of everything and nobody owns anything
  • You need to hire to grow but every new employee feels like a gamble

Problems We Solve

Hiring problems are rarely just hiring problems. They are system problems.

  • High turnover that disrupts operations and burns time and money
  • No consistent process for screening and evaluating candidates
  • New hires are thrown into work without proper training or expectations
  • Team members do not have clear job descriptions or performance standards
  • The owner is still doing work that should be handled by employees
  • There is no management layer -- the owner manages everyone directly
  • Good employees leave because they do not see a future or feel unsupported
  • Every departure creates a scramble to fill the gap

What We Deliver

Everything you need to hire well, onboard effectively, and build a team that sticks.

  • Job descriptions and role definitions that set clear expectations
  • Hiring process -- from job posting to screening to interview to offer
  • Interview frameworks that help you evaluate fit beyond gut feeling
  • Onboarding system -- structured first 30, 60, and 90 days
  • Training materials and SOPs for each role
  • Performance review framework your managers can actually use
  • Org chart and team structure design for your current and next stage of growth
  • Management coaching to help you or your leads develop as people managers

How It Works

From hiring chaos to a team-building system that works every time.

Team Assessment

We evaluate your current team structure, role definitions, hiring history, and management practices. We identify where the breakdowns are happening and why.

Role and Structure Design

We define clear roles, build an org chart that supports your growth plans, and create job descriptions that attract the right candidates.

Hiring System Build

We create the repeatable process -- posting templates, screening criteria, interview questions, evaluation scorecards, and offer templates. A system your team can run every time you hire.

Onboarding and Training

We build the onboarding sequence and training materials that get new hires productive fast and set them up for long-term success.

Tools and Systems

We select tools based on your team size and budget -- small businesses do not need enterprise HR software.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have tried hiring and people just do not work out. What would be different?

Most hiring failures in small businesses come from unclear expectations, no real onboarding, and no system for evaluating fit before making an offer. We build structure around every stage -- from how you write the job posting to how you screen candidates to how you onboard and train them in the first 90 days. When the process is repeatable and clear, you make better hires.

Do you recruit or place candidates for us?

No. We are not a staffing agency. We build the hiring system your business uses -- the job descriptions, screening process, interview framework, onboarding sequence, and training materials. You or your team runs the system. We make sure it works.

How do you help with retention, not just hiring?

Retention starts before someone is hired -- with clear expectations, proper onboarding, and a real role definition. We also help you build feedback systems, career paths where applicable, and management practices that keep good people engaged. Most small business turnover is not about pay. It is about clarity, communication, and feeling like they matter.

What if we only have a few employees?

Team building systems are actually most important when you are small. Every hire matters more because each person represents a larger percentage of your capacity. Getting the right people in the right seats with clear roles is critical when you have a team of 5 just as much as when you have 50. The systems we build are sized for your current team and scalable as you grow.

How long does it take to build a hiring system?

A basic hiring and onboarding system can be built in 3-4 weeks. A more comprehensive engagement that includes role redesign, team restructuring, and management training typically takes 6-8 weeks. If you have an immediate hiring need, we can fast-track the core pieces you need to make a strong hire right now.
