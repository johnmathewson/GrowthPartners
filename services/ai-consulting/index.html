Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI readiness assessment?

An AI readiness assessment evaluates your current business processes, technology stack, team capabilities, and data quality to determine where AI can deliver meaningful results. We look at what you are doing manually that could be automated, what tools you already have, and where the biggest opportunities are for time and cost savings.

How is AI consulting different from AI implementation?

AI consulting is the strategy phase -- we assess your business, identify opportunities, and build a prioritized roadmap. AI implementation is the execution phase where we actually configure tools and build automations. Many clients start with consulting to get clarity, then move into implementation with confidence.

Do you recommend specific AI tools or are you vendor-neutral?

We are completely vendor-neutral. We do not resell software or earn commissions from tool companies. Our recommendations are based entirely on what fits your business, your budget, and your team's ability to use the tools day to day.

How much does AI consulting cost for a small business?

Costs depend on the scope of your business and the depth of assessment needed. We offer a free initial consultation to understand your situation. From there, we can scope a consulting engagement that fits your budget. Most small business owners find that the clarity alone saves them from making expensive tool purchases that would not have worked.

We are not a tech company. Is AI consulting still relevant for us?

Absolutely. AI consulting is most valuable for non-tech businesses because there is so much noise in the market. Service businesses like HVAC companies, restaurants, landscapers, and property managers all have repetitive processes that benefit from AI. The consulting engagement helps you cut through the hype and focus on what will actually move the needle.
