Frequently Asked Questions

What is a marketing system versus a marketing campaign?

A campaign is a one-time push -- an ad, a promotion, an event. A marketing system is a repeatable process that consistently generates leads without starting from scratch each time. It includes your brand positioning, lead capture mechanisms, nurture sequences, referral processes, and follow-up automation. Systems compound over time. Campaigns end.

Do you run ads or manage social media for us?

We focus on building the marketing strategy and systems, not day-to-day execution of ads or social media posts. We can set up the infrastructure, define the strategy, and connect you with execution partners if needed. Our goal is to make sure you have a system that works regardless of who is pressing the buttons.

How do you help with branding without a full rebrand?

Most service businesses do not need a full rebrand. They need clarity -- a clear message, consistent positioning, and a brand voice that resonates with their target customer. We help you define who you serve, what makes you different, and how to communicate that consistently across every touchpoint.

We get most of our business from referrals. Do we still need marketing systems?

Referrals are great, but they are unpredictable. A marketing system gives you a second and third channel for leads so you are not entirely dependent on word of mouth. It also helps you systematize your referral process -- making it proactive instead of passive -- so you get more of the referrals you are already earning.
