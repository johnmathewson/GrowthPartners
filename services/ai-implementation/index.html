Frequently Asked Questions

How long does AI implementation take for a small business?

Most implementations take 4-8 weeks from audit to full deployment. The timeline depends on the complexity of your workflows and how many processes we are automating. We start with quick wins that deliver value in week one, then layer in more sophisticated automation over time.

Do we need technical staff to maintain AI tools?

No. We specifically choose and configure tools that your existing team can manage. Part of our implementation includes hands-on training so your people are comfortable making adjustments. We also provide 30 days of support after launch to handle any questions.

What kind of ROI can we expect from AI implementation?

Results vary by business, but the most common outcome is recovering 10-15 hours per week of administrative time through automated follow-up, scheduling, and data entry. Many owners also see improved lead response times and fewer dropped balls in their sales process.

What if we already use some software tools but they are not connected?

That is one of the most common situations we see. Most small businesses have 3-5 tools that do not talk to each other, creating manual work and data gaps. A big part of our implementation work is connecting your existing tools so data flows automatically between them.

Is AI implementation only for tech-savvy business owners?

Not at all. Most of our clients are hands-on operators who are good at their trade but not particularly technical. We handle the configuration and integration work. You just need to know your business well enough to tell us what is working and what is not.
