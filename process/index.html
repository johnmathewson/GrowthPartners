How Growth Partners Works

Three phases. No jargon. No mystery. Here's exactly what happens when you work with us — from the first conversation to a business that runs without you.

The Short Version

We assess where your business stands today. We build a prioritized plan based on what has the highest leverage. Then we implement alongside you — systems, AI tools, processes, training — until it's running and your team owns it.

Assess

Understand what's working, what isn't, and where you're stuck.

Plan

Build a prioritized roadmap based on impact, not theory.

Build

Implement the systems, tools, and processes that change how your business runs.

Phase 1: Assess

Before we can fix anything, we need to understand how your business actually operates — not how you think it operates.

What This Includes

  • Deep-dive conversation with you about every aspect of the business
  • Review of your current systems (or lack thereof)
  • Assessment of your team structure, roles, and capacity
  • Analysis of your revenue model, margins, and financial visibility
  • Identification of the top bottlenecks holding you back
  • Evaluation of AI and automation opportunities

What You Get

A clear, honest picture of where your business stands — what's working, what's broken, and what has the highest leverage for improvement. No sugarcoating.

This isn't a generic audit. It's a working assessment built around your specific business, your specific problems, and your specific goals.

Timeline

The assessment phase typically takes 1 to 2 weeks, depending on the complexity of your business.

Phase 2: Plan

With the assessment complete, we build a roadmap that prioritizes by impact — tackling the things that will make the biggest difference first.

What This Includes

  • Prioritized action plan organized by phase
  • Specific recommendations for systems, tools, and processes
  • AI and automation strategy tailored to your operations
  • Team development and hiring recommendations
  • Timeline and milestones for implementation
  • Investment requirements and expected outcomes

What You Get

A practical roadmap you can actually execute. Not a 50-page strategy document that sits in a drawer. Each recommendation includes the what, why, how, and when — so you always know what's next and why it matters.

We walk through the plan together. You ask questions. We adjust. Nothing moves forward until you're clear and comfortable.

Timeline

The planning phase typically takes 1 to 2 weeks after the assessment is complete.

Phase 3: Build

This is where most consultants stop and we keep going. We don't just tell you what to do — we build it with you.

What This Includes

  • Implementation of systems and standard operating procedures
  • AI tool setup, configuration, and integration
  • CRM and automation buildout
  • Team training and onboarding to new systems
  • Process documentation your team can follow
  • Ongoing check-ins to ensure adoption and results

What You Get

A business that runs differently than it did before. Systems your team actually uses. AI tools that save real time. Processes that work whether you're watching or not.

We stay engaged through the build phase to make sure everything sticks. We're not done until your team is running the new systems confidently on their own.

Timeline

The build phase varies depending on scope — typically 30 to 90 days for the initial implementation. Many clients continue with ongoing advisory after the core build is complete.

After the Build: Ongoing Advisory

Many of our clients continue working with us on an ongoing basis after the core systems are in place. This looks like regular check-ins, strategic guidance, help with new challenges, and continued optimization as the business grows.

It's not required — some clients complete the build phase and have everything they need. But if you want a thought partner as you continue to scale, we're here.
