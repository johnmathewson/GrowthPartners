Pricing & Engagement Models

Straightforward ways to work together. No hidden fees, no long-term lock-ins. Pick the model that fits where you are right now.

How We Work Together

Every business is different, so we don't force a one-size-fits-all package. Here are the three ways most clients engage with us.

Business Assessment

A deep-dive evaluation of your business as it runs today. We look at your systems, team, operations, financials, and growth opportunities — then deliver a prioritized report of what to fix and in what order.

  • Comprehensive operational review
  • AI and automation opportunity analysis
  • Team and organizational assessment
  • Prioritized action plan you can execute

Best for: Business owners who want clarity on what needs to change before committing to a larger engagement.

Timeline: 1-2 weeks

Project-Based Implementation

A defined scope of work with clear deliverables and a timeline. We work alongside you and your team to build specific systems, deploy AI tools, or solve targeted problems.

  • Defined scope, timeline, and deliverables
  • Hands-on implementation (not just strategy)
  • Team training and documentation
  • Follow-up to ensure adoption

Best for: Business owners who know what needs to be built and want help implementing it right.

Timeline: 30-90 days depending on scope

Ongoing Advisory Retainer

A monthly partnership where we serve as your strategic advisor and implementation partner. Regular check-ins, ongoing support, and hands-on help as your business evolves.

  • Regular strategic check-ins
  • Ongoing implementation support
  • Priority access for new challenges
  • Continuous optimization of existing systems

Best for: Business owners who want an ongoing thought partner and implementation resource as they scale.

Timeline: Month-to-month, no long-term contract required

How Pricing Works

We don't publish fixed prices because every engagement is scoped to the specific needs of your business. A restaurant that needs CRM automation is a very different project from a construction company that needs a complete operational overhaul.

Here's what we can tell you:

  • We discuss pricing openly during the free consultation — no surprises
  • Every engagement has a clearly defined scope and investment before we start
  • We don't do open-ended billing — you'll always know what you're paying and what you're getting
  • Our clients are $1M-$10M businesses — our pricing reflects that market
  • We'd rather scope the right engagement than sell you more than you need

The best way to get specific pricing is to book a free consultation. We'll learn about your business and give you a clear picture of what an engagement would look like and cost.

Pricing FAQ

How much does Growth Partners consulting cost?

Pricing varies based on the scope of work. We offer three engagement models: a standalone business assessment, project-based implementation with a defined scope and timeline, and ongoing monthly advisory retainers. We'll discuss pricing specifics during your free consultation after we understand your business and needs.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. Our project-based work has a defined scope and end date. Our ongoing advisory retainers are month-to-month — you stay because it's working, not because you're locked in.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes. The initial consultation is a real conversation about your business — no charge, no obligation. We use it to understand where you are and whether we're a good fit to help.

What's included in a business assessment?

The assessment includes a thorough review of your operations, systems, team, financials, and growth opportunities. You receive a detailed report with prioritized recommendations — actionable steps you can take whether or not you continue working with us.

Can I start with an assessment and decide later about implementation?

Absolutely. Many clients start with the assessment to get clarity, then decide on implementation afterward. The assessment stands on its own as a valuable deliverable.
