Serving Small Businesses Across Northwest Indiana

We work with owner-operated service businesses throughout Lake and Porter County. Local knowledge, hands-on implementation, real results for NWI businesses.

Our Service Area

Growth Partners is rooted in Northwest Indiana. We serve small businesses across Lake County and Porter County — from Hammond in the north to Lowell in the south, and from Highland in the west to Chesterton in the east.

We know this region. We understand the industries that drive it, the challenges that NWI business owners face, and what it takes to build something here. When you work with us, you're working with people who are part of this community — not a national firm running a playbook.

While our core service area is NWI, much of our work — especially AI implementation and consulting — can be delivered remotely. If you're outside the region and think we might be a fit, reach out.
