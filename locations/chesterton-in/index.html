How Chesterton Businesses Are Using AI and Systems

Here is what practical AI and systemization look like for businesses in Chesterton's core industries.

Boutique Retail: Inventory & Customer Follow-Up Automation

A Chesterton boutique managing hundreds of SKUs across seasons can use automated inventory tracking to flag reorder points and prevent dead stock. Pair that with AI-driven customer follow-up — personalized messages after a purchase, birthday reminders, new arrival alerts — and you keep customers coming back without manually tracking every interaction.

Restaurant: Staff Scheduling & Food Cost Tracking

When summer tourism doubles your covers but your scheduling still runs on a whiteboard and group texts, labor costs spiral. Automated scheduling matched to sales forecasts keeps you staffed right. Integrated food cost tracking shows actual plate cost versus menu price so you stop guessing whether you are making money on each dish.

Event Venue: Automated Booking & Client Communication

A Porter County event space fielding dozens of inquiries a week can use automated booking workflows to capture leads, send proposals, collect deposits, and manage the entire client communication timeline — from first inquiry through post-event follow-up — without the owner manually managing every email and phone call.
