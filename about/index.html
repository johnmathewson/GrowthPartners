About Growth Partners

We help small business owners who have built a job instead of a business. We're the people who show up, roll up our sleeves, and build the systems that set you free.

Our Story

Growth Partners exists because we've seen the same story play out hundreds of times across Northwest Indiana. A skilled person starts a business. They're good at what they do, so the business grows. But growth without systems creates a trap — the owner becomes the most overworked, underpaid employee in the company.

We started Growth Partners to solve that specific problem. Not with theory or motivational platitudes, but with the actual infrastructure that businesses need to operate without the owner being involved in every decision.

We work with owner-operated service businesses doing $1M to $10M in revenue. These are real businesses with real employees and real customers — but they've hit the ceiling that comes from running everything through one person.

How We Think About This Work

Servant Leadership

We believe the best leaders build people up. Our job isn't to make you dependent on us — it's to build the systems and capabilities inside your business so you and your team can run things on your own. We succeed when you don't need us anymore.

Practical, Not Hype

We don't sell transformation. We build systems. There's no magic framework, no secret formula. Just clear-headed assessment of where you are, what needs to change, and the discipline to actually implement it.

Building People

Systems only work when the people running them are set up to succeed. A huge part of what we do is helping your team develop the skills, habits, and accountability structures they need to operate at a higher level.

Implementation Over Advice

The world doesn't need more consultants who hand you a binder full of recommendations and disappear. We implement. We build. We stay until the systems are running and your team owns them.

Who We Work With

We're a good fit if this sounds like you.

  • You run a service business doing $1M to $10M in revenue
  • You're the bottleneck for most decisions
  • Your team is good, but they rely on you too much
  • You know you need better systems but aren't sure where to start
  • You're interested in AI but don't want to waste money on the wrong tools
  • You want to grow but not at the expense of your sanity
  • You're willing to invest in building real infrastructure
  • You're based in or near Northwest Indiana

How We're Different

Most consulting firms are built around selling you a process. They have a methodology, a framework, a branded system — and they fit your business into it whether it belongs there or not.

We start with your business as it actually operates. We look at what's working, what isn't, and what has the highest leverage for change. Then we build around that reality.

We also don't separate strategy from implementation. The same people who assess your business and design the solutions are the ones who build them, train your team, and stick around to make sure everything works.

And we're local. We're in Northwest Indiana, working with businesses in Lake and Porter County. We understand this market, these industries, and what it takes to build something here.
