Book a Free Consultation

A focused conversation about your business — where you are, what's not working, and what to do about it. No pitch. No obligation.

What to Expect

This isn't a sales call disguised as a consultation. It's a real conversation about your business with someone who understands what it takes to run one.

Here's what happens during the call:

  • We learn about your business — what you do, how it runs today, and where you're stuck
  • We identify the biggest bottlenecks and highest-leverage opportunities
  • We give you an honest assessment of what needs to change and in what order
  • If we're a fit to help, we'll outline what working together would look like
  • If we're not a fit, we'll tell you that too — and point you in the right direction

The call typically runs 30 to 45 minutes. You'll walk away with clarity on your next steps regardless of whether we work together.

Who This Is For

This consultation is designed for owner-operators of service businesses in the $1M to $10M revenue range. You're a good fit if:

  • You own a service business in or near Northwest Indiana
  • You're doing $1M+ in revenue but feeling stuck
  • You're the bottleneck for too many decisions
  • You want to build systems but don't know where to start
  • You're interested in using AI to streamline operations
  • You're ready to invest in building real business infrastructure
