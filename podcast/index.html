The Show

The podcast for builders, not spectators

Northwest Indiana is full of people quietly doing remarkable things. Roofers who built empires from a single truck. Restaurant owners who survived everything. Contractors who turned a side hustle into a 30-person operation.

Growth Conversations is where those stories get told. No fluff, no hype — just honest conversations about what it actually takes to build something real in this region.

The Studio

Recorded in Northwest Indiana

We record at the Growth Partners studio — a purpose-built podcast space designed to feel like a conversation, not an interview. Two green leather chairs, professional mics, and a room that makes people comfortable enough to be honest.

  • Professional audio — broadcast-quality microphones
  • Video recorded — available on YouTube
  • Relaxed, conversational format — no scripts
  • Typically 45–60 minutes, edited to the best parts
  • Guest receives a copy of their episode
  • Promoted across Growth Partners social channels
