How We Helped an Event Venue Build a Fully Autonomous Business System

Wildwoods Event Venue in Northwest Indiana was growing fast -- but every lead, booking, and detail ran through the owner. We built the systems, trained the team, and implemented AI automation to free the owner from daily operations.

The Problem

The owner of Wildwoods Event Venue was personally managing every aspect of the business -- from initial lead inquiries to kitchen recipes to post-event follow-up. There were no documented systems in place. Every lead, every booking, and every operational detail ran through her directly.

The business was growing, but the owner had hit a ceiling. There were only so many hours in a day, and she was spending all of them working in the business rather than on it. Scaling was impossible without fundamental changes to how the operation ran.

The Plan

We started with a full operations audit, mapping every touchpoint in the business from the moment a lead came in through post-event follow-up. The goal was to systematize each step with documented processes, train the team to execute independently, and then layer in AI-powered automation to handle the repetitive work.

This was not about adding technology for the sake of it. It was about building a business that could run without the owner being involved in every decision.

What Was Implemented

Phase 1: Full Business Systemization

We documented standard operating procedures for every core process in the business. We built accountability frameworks so the team knew who owned what. Then we trained the team on every system until they could execute without the owner's involvement.

Phase 2: AI-Powered CRM

With systems in place, we built an automated lead-to-booking pipeline using an AI CRM. This included AI-driven communication for lead follow-up, integrated scheduling, and automated workflows that moved prospects through the booking process without manual intervention.

Phase 3: Fully Autonomous System (In Progress)

The current phase is building a fully autonomous system that handles everything from the first inquiry to kitchen prep to post-event follow-up. The goal is a business where the owner can step away and the operation continues without missing a beat.

Outcomes

  • Steady, predictable lead flow replacing ad hoc inquiries
  • Owner freed from daily operations and able to focus on growth
  • Team operating independently using documented systems
  • AI CRM handling lead follow-up and booking communication automatically
  • Business positioned to scale without adding owner hours

Tools Used

GoHighLevel, AI automation tools, and custom workflow systems -- all configured specifically for Wildwoods' operations and integrated into their existing processes.

Who This Is Relevant For

This engagement is relevant for event venues, hospitality businesses, and any service business with complex multi-step operations where the owner is involved in every detail. If your business depends on you being present for things to get done, the approach we used here applies directly.
