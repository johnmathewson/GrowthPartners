Case Studies & Results

Real businesses. Real challenges. Here's what it looks like when an owner-operated service business gets the right systems, AI tools, and strategies in place.

Wildwoods Event Venue

Industry: Event Venues  |  Location: Northwest Indiana

The Challenge

Wildwoods is an event venue that was growing fast but drowning in manual processes. Every inquiry, every booking, every vendor coordination ran through the owner. Leads were falling through the cracks. The team had no documented systems for handling events from inquiry to execution. The owner was working seven days a week and still felt behind.

What We Did

  • Implemented an AI-powered CRM to capture and automatically follow up with every lead
  • Built automated booking workflows that moved prospects from inquiry to contract without manual chasing
  • Created documented SOPs for event setup, coordination, and teardown
  • Set up AI-assisted customer communication for common questions and scheduling
  • Trained the team to run the new systems independently

The Outcome

The venue now has a functioning lead pipeline that operates without the owner touching every inquiry. The team has clear processes for every phase of event management. The owner is no longer the single point of failure for daily operations.

Commercial Construction Company

Industry: Commercial Construction  |  Location: Northwest Indiana

The Challenge

A growing commercial construction business where the owner was involved in every estimate, every project schedule, and every client call. The company had the revenue and the crew to handle more work, but the owner was the bottleneck for everything. No project management system. No estimating process that didn't require the owner. No delegation structure.

What We Did

  • Built a systemized estimating process that senior team members could handle
  • Implemented project management systems for scheduling, tracking, and client updates
  • Created clear role definitions and accountability structures for the team
  • Set up AI tools for document management and communication automation
  • Developed an onboarding system for new project managers

The Outcome

The owner moved from being involved in every project detail to overseeing the business at a strategic level. The team now handles estimating, project management, and client communication through documented systems. The company is positioned to take on more projects without the owner working more hours.

Multi-Location Restaurant

Industry: Restaurants  |  Location: Northwest Indiana

The Challenge

A restaurant owner expanding from one successful location to multiple sites. The first location ran well because the owner was there every day. But that model doesn't scale to two or three locations. There was no operational playbook, no consistent training process, and no way to maintain quality without the owner's physical presence.

What We Did

  • Built a comprehensive operations playbook covering every position and process
  • Implemented AI-powered customer feedback and reputation management systems
  • Created management structures and accountability systems for each location
  • Set up financial dashboards for multi-location visibility
  • Developed hiring and training systems that maintain quality standards across sites

The Outcome

The restaurant now operates with documented systems at every location. Managers run day-to-day operations following clear processes. The owner has visibility into all locations through dashboards and reporting, without needing to be physically present at every site.

The Pattern

The solution is also the same in every case: build the systems, implement the right tools, and develop the team so the business operates at a level that doesn't depend on one person doing everything.
